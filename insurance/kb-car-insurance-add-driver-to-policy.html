Adding a driver to an existing
car insurance policy

When to add a driver

Are you deciding whether to add a driver to your insurance policy? It can be confusing! Adding drivers to your policy, when appropriate, could help you avoid negative consequences in the event of a lending loss. The information below can help guide you in making the right decision.

If I add a driver will it affect my premium or rate?

In some cases, adding a driver to your policy can affect your premium, but not always. Many factors determine the cost of adding a driver, like driver relationship, age, driving history, past claims, and driving records.

For example, adding a newly licensed teen driver with little driving experience can increase the premium, but a good student discount may be able to help offset that difference. Regardless of the type of driver you are adding to your policy, experienced drivers may be eligible for additional discounts, as most insurance companies have discounts for good drivers.

How to Add a Driver

Already a Qinsure customer? Add a driver by logging in to your account or mobile app. New customers can get a quote online to start a policy.

Once logged into your My Qinsure Policy Portal simply follow the below steps:

1. On the Auto policy you wish to amend, select the 'I would like To...' box and choose 'Add Vehicle'.

2. Provide details of the vehicle you want to add to your policy for the additional driver. Click the 'Continue' button.

3. On the 'Vehicle Usage' page, scroll down to the 'Vehicle Assignment' section where you will see the current members of your household associated with your policy. Click the 'Edit' button to add an additional household member.

4. Provide the name of your additional household member you would like to add to your Auto policy as it appears on their drivers license. Then click the 'Add Driver' button.

5. Back on the 'Vehicle Usage' page, you will now see your addtional household member available for selection under the 'Who will drive it the most' selection list. Provide details on the Primary Use of the new vehicle and the estimated annual mileage and then click 'Save'.

6. You will see your new policy for your new vehicle and you additional household member added to your My Qinsure Policy Portal home page.
