FAQs

1. What are Inclusive Extras?
Inclusive Extras are amazing benefits included in our Ultimate and Challenger plans. Choose the Inclusive Extra that’s right for you from Apple Music to BT Sport and do more of what you love. Apple One is automatically included when you select the Ultimate plan for iPhone, we'll even throw in our Browse Abroad Pass. If you select a Challenger plan, you can pay an extra £5 per month to select Apple One as an Inclusive Extra.
2. How can I check what Inclusive Extra is active?
You can check which Inclusive Extra is active on your plan by heading to the 'Plan, Inclusive Extras & Plan Add-ons' section of the Dolphin Telco app, available on the iOS App Store and on the Google Play store. You can also check on My Dolphin online, by texting SMART to 850 or by speaking to a Dolphin Telco advisor.

3. Can I get more Inclusive Extras?
Many of our Inclusive Extras are also available as a standalone add-ons that can be added and removed from your plan at any time. You can add additional add-ons to your plan through the 'Add-ons' section of the Dolphin Telco app and on My Dolphin Telco online.
